Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment? +

You can book through our website form above, call us at 9843805588 or 9823207031, or message us directly on WhatsApp. We confirm all bookings within 2 hours.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, eSewa (9843805588), and bank transfer. Payment is collected after the service is completed.

How do I enroll in a course? +

Fill out the enrollment form on our website, download the form from the Downloads section, or visit our academy directly in New Baneshwor. Our team will guide you through the enrollment process.

Are the certificates internationally recognized? +

Yes, our certificates are recognized by beauty industry professionals and establishments across Nepal and are accepted by many international institutions. We provide certified training aligned with international beauty standards.

Do you offer home service or home visits? +

Yes, we offer home visits for bridal services and special occasions within Kathmandu Valley. Please call in advance to check availability and pricing.

What products do you use? +

We use premium, professional-grade products from brands like Schwarzkopf, L'Oreal Professional, and Wella. All products are dermatologically tested and skin-safe.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment? +

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your appointment without any charge. Please WhatsApp or call us to make changes.
